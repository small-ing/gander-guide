Welcome to aiSL, an ASL RealTime Recognition Project

By Turbo Tornadoes from AI Camp

Welcome to aiSL, a model dedicated to providing users with a free online service that can translate American Sign Language into the english alphabet with realtime video footage. Trained on more than 71,000 images, aiSL, through the help of extensive AI models and python libraries, is revolutionary in bringing services that are currently being done with the help of human operators and paid services.
